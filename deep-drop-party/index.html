About the Show

Seven years. Every Saturday. No exceptions.

The Deep Drop Party is a weekly live broadcast on Discord featuring the audio art of Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns — immersive audio, devotional sound work, and narrative unfolding across months and years.

Each three-hour episode is co-hosted by showrunner Bliss Blank and a rotating cast of guest star co-hostesses — voice performers who have starred in Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns productions and return to curate, narrate, and inhabit the work live.

The Deep Drop Party is built around consensual participation, mutual respect, and voluntary engagement. Some listeners treat it like late-night radio. Others become part of the evolving community around the work. The show is one layer of a larger body of work — every episode a live traversal of the Neuralverse.

The Deep Drop Party functions simultaneously as live performance, archival curation, and commentary layer across the Neuralverse catalogue — every episode is a live traversal of the body of work.

Formats

What occurs in the show

Each episode draws from a range of formats — sometimes in combination, sometimes pure. The mix varies week to week and is shaped by the co-hostess, the theme, and the work being featured.

◈
Curated DJ Set

A sequenced playlist of Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns tracks, built around a theme or arc. The backbone of every episode.

◉
World Premiere

New tracks debut on the show before appearing in the catalogue. Premieres are flagged in the episode archive.

▽
Live Narrative

Running storylines and character arcs that unfold across episodes — some evolving into full podcast audio dramas.

⬡
Haptic Session

Final tracks in each episode feature XToys haptic integration — sensation programmed directly into the audio. Voluntary.

◌
Community Listening

Live chat, hostess interaction, and shared experience in real time. Lurking and active participation are equally welcome.

⬟
Archive Show

Deep dives into older catalogue material — revisiting series arcs, connecting early work to recent developments.

Structure

Recurring segments

Every episode follows a loose structure. Specific tracks and themes change weekly; the shape of the evening stays consistent.

01
Opening — Mind Popper

The show's recurring opening track. Sets the tone and signals the start of the broadcast.

02–06
Main Set

Four to six tracks curated around the episode's theme, arc, or featured co-hostess. May include premieres, narrative audio, or catalogue deep cuts.

Final
Haptic Closing Tracks

The final one to two tracks feature XToys haptic integration. Audience participation via typed trigger phrases in Discord chat.

Participation

How listeners engage

There is no single right way to be at the Deep Drop Party. The show accommodates every mode from silent listening to active co-creation.

Just listen

Join the Discord audio channel and listen like radio. No chat required, no participation pressure. The show works as pure audio.

Join the chat

The hostesses read and respond to chat in real time. Drop in when you feel like it. The Pink Room community builds relationships across episodes.

Haptic participation

Connect a personal massager via XToys and the final tracks become physical. Audience members trigger haptics by typing phrases in chat. Android/desktop only. Entirely voluntary.
