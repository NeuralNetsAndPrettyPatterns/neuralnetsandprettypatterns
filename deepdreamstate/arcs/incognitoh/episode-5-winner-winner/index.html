Summary

The final rounds push the remaining contestants to breaking points. Footage from their booths gets released, revealing secrets and destroying alliances.

They're asked to choose between each other. The architects want them to demonstrate that they've been fully converted into agents of the system. The Council's past subtle. They openly manipulate outcomes. They show how judges vote and how all of it has been designed from the beginning. Everyone’s mask is coming off, and the game’s stripping down to the machinery of control.

As the elimination rounds accelerate, secret footage from the contestants' private booths is released. One by one, they eliminate each other, performing betrayal because performance is what they are now.
