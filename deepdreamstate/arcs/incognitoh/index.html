There’s a world behind your games.

Incognitoh is pure pink noir. The cage is a dollhouse and the leashes have names on them. They’re both very sparkly.

The surveillance system is designed to look like a game show, and everyone inside it chose their room color. Five contestants move into the Glass House, a fully transparent competition space. Going private looks suspicious. Visibility feels like winning.

The Uniforms are form-fitting and haptic. It’s a panopticon with a hyperpop soundtrack.

The prize is real and permanent. It’s older and more permanent than cash.

Human Made Art

Deep Dream State uses human art direction and human creative decision-making throughout the visual and audio supply chain. Arc artwork is curated as part of the Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns archive.

Consent Declaration

Deep Dream State is a desire horror audio drama written and produced by Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns. It explores psychological fiction at the boundaries of control, identity, surveillance, and complicity. All performances are works of fiction and take place within a consensual creative context.

Incognitoh contains surveillance themes, reality competition horror, identity manipulation, adult themes, haptic uniform imagery, coercive game systems, and stylized psychological control.
