Why aren't you in uniform?

Summary

The right fit changes everything. Glass House uniforms take fit to the next level.

They’re sleek, form-fitting Heavy Metal fantasies, embedded with haptic feedback systems and biometric sensors. Each uniform is personalized and calibrated. When a contestant performs, the uniform generates sensations. The uniforms also extract data in real time. Every heartbeat and every moment of arousal is being recorded, analyzed, and used to calibrate the next challenge.

The contestants discover that these sensations are addictive. Within hours, they crave them. They begin to compete for the next reward. Hannah, who was quiet and observant, suddenly becomes aggressive. Madison abandons strategic alliances in favor of individual performance. Ashley streams it all, performing compliance for cameras and uniforms.

The uniforms do more than enforce the game, though. They are the game. Each one displays the contestants’ secret icons in the secret places. In the Glass House, seduction’s the most important strategy.
