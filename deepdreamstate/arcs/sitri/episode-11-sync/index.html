Summary

False awakening might be cheap, but that’s how we got the six pack at Dream Costco.

When the scene shifts, Meg and Tessa surface into what appears to be the observation room. Their roles have shifted though. The uniforms have changed. Their titles are fever dream fantasies. They can see what they’ve become; the monitors display every subject’s fantasy about them as they walk through the Center.

In this timeline, Sitri changed along one crucial axis. It scaled. The dreamer become hundreds of dreamers and the lab became a warehouse.

Elle’s shifted as well. They can’t help but stare at her, and when they do, they can see the seams.
