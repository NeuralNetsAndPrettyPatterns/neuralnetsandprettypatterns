The Archive

Welcome to the Neuralverse

Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns has created an enormous collection of interconnected audio content. This catalogue presents over 655 original works spanning audio drama, music, narrative audio, and groundbreaking experiments with haptic technology.

The Neuralverse is unique. It is an interconnected series of productions developed over years of imaginal work. This catalogue maps many of those connections, but there will always be more to find and experience. The Deep Dream State audio drama is one part of this universe — the tip of a much larger iceberg.

This page serves as the official artist-approved discography. Each entry is identified by a distinct NNPPID — a permanent identifier unique to this catalogue system.

Navigation

The Map

This catalogue is organized in three ways:

  1. A list of series below. Tracks are grouped by theme or setting. Some series are linear narratives. Others share a setting or a recurring element. Each series links to its own page.
  2. A set of facets. These describe properties of individual tracks — voice, format, haptic integration — rather than narrative groupings.
  3. A chronological archive. The complete track list runs in order of release, 50 tracks per page. You are on page 1 of 14.
Consent & Content Notice

Audience Notice

Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns is deeply committed to ethical practice. All audio content is created and performed by consenting adults within a carefully articulated ethical framework. This catalogue is designed for audiences who actively choose to engage. Enthusiastic, informed consent is foundational to every project.

Fictional depiction is not endorsement. These audio experiences are imaginative explorations. All scenarios occur between consenting participants within fantasy frames.
