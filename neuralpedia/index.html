What is this?

The Neuralverse is the complete body of work by Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns: over 700 original pieces of audio drama, music, and immersive sound, built over years and all quietly wired together. Deep Dream State is just the visible tip.

Every entry carries a permanent NNPPID, so nothing in the catalogue ever gets lost or renamed out from under you.

How do I navigate this?

Three ways in:

  1. By series, below. Some are linear stories, others share a setting or a recurring element. Each links to its own page.
  2. By facet, below. These describe properties of a track (voice, format, haptic integration) rather than a narrative grouping.
  3. Chronologically, via the complete track list in release order. Use search above for the fastest way to find something specific.
Is this ethical? Is this a cult?

No. Neural Nets and Pretty Patterns is deeply committed to ethical practice. All audio content is created and performed by consenting adults within a carefully articulated ethical framework, for audiences who actively choose to engage. Enthusiastic, informed consent is foundational to every project.

Fictional depiction is not endorsement. These audio experiences are imaginative explorations. All scenarios occur between consenting participants within fantasy frames.

What are the series?

Series are the narrative and thematic groupings inside the Neuralverse. Some are ongoing storylines, others are a shared setting or recurring device revisited across many tracks. There are 70 in total, ranging from long-running flagship series to one-off experiments.

What are the facets?

Facets describe what a track actually is: its format and delivery, rather than which story it belongs to. Use them alongside series to narrow things down. A facet plus a series gets you close to exactly what you're after.
