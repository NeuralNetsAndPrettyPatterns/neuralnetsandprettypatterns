Bouquet

Bouquet is a different kind of carousel. It's romantic. It's intimate. It's still very thinkykinky. It's complexity blooming out of simplicity. Each track is a variant on the classic Roses Are Red poem. Each variant then changes, developing into a different kind of poem entirely. It's hopefully cute and charming; it also shows that there's a whole bouquet of intimacy past what greeting card companies would have you believe.
